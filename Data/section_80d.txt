Section 80D - Deduction for Medical Insurance Premium

Section 80D allows a deduction for premiums paid towards health insurance policies, available only under the Old Tax Regime.

Deduction limits:
- Premium paid for self, spouse, and dependent children: up to Rs 25,000 per year
- If the taxpayer is a senior citizen (60 years or above): up to Rs 50,000 per year
- Premium paid for parents: an additional Rs 25,000 per year (Rs 50,000 if parents are senior citizens)
- Preventive health check-up expenses: up to Rs 5,000 within the overall limit

Maximum possible deduction: Rs 1,00,000 per year (if both the taxpayer and parents are senior citizens).

Section 80D deductions are NOT available under the New Tax Regime (Section 115BAC).
